Reduce the noise on the Data and boost confidence among User communities.

**Business Case -**
Lack of appropriate tools to measure data quality on Big Data Lake leading to poor adoption of data and low confidence on Data.

**Solution -**
Ideated, championed 0-1 Product Launch that enabled single click self-serve enterprise data quality tool enabling Data Stewards and Custodians to proactively detect anomalies and enforce data integrity standards, achieving over 90% accuracy across enterprise datasets.

**Outcome -**
User by over 3k Data Stewards and Custodians across enterprise, actively scanning 30 PB scale Data and checking Anomalies on over 100K CDEs.
